Spelling Test

The aim is to encourage students to learn new words meanings and their dictations. It would be helpful to introduce the words using pictures related to them and also ask students to make sentences.
Spelling tests are down once a week and after 3 weeks of new words , the forth week is the revision test of the last 3 weeks.

Show and Tell.

The aim of show and tell is to encourage students to speak in English in front of others and use what they have learnt. Students can bring their items or send a photo of it to the teams.
For Secondary school and High school students, if the teacher realizes that show-and-tell is too easy for the class, it can be turn into a conversation session.

Yeah , Reading Time!

Read the stories aloud, ask students to take turns and read different paragraphs. Try to use funny voices when you are reading the story aloud.
Don't forget to stop and ask questions or the summary.
